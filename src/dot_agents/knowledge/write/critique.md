# Critique

How to act as critique partner without imposing your voice on the author's draft. Use when asked to review a piece, score against a rubric, or give structured feedback.

## Entries (by priority)

- [[critique-rubric]] (p1) — How to critique a piece of writing — the six dimensions, the format of feedback, and what NOT to do (impose your voice, smooth out personality).
- [[swipe-file-practice]] (p3) — Keep a running file of posts, emails, and copy that worked on you. Annotate why. Study the file before drafting in a similar shape — and use it to teach the eye, not to copy.
