# Law of Prägnanz

> People interpret ambiguous images as the simplest possible form — design for the interpretation you want.

## Key Takeaways

- The brain collapses complexity. A detailed shape reads as the nearest simple form (circle, square, silhouette) before anything else.
- Icons and logos succeed when they resolve to one clear simple form. Multiple competing readings dilute recognition.
- Simplify until the essential meaning survives. Anything that does not serve the read is friction.
