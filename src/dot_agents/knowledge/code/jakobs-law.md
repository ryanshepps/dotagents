# Jakob's Law

> Users spend most of their time on other sites — they expect yours to work the same way.

## Key Takeaways

- Novelty is a tax. Users carry mental models from every other product they use; matching those conventions makes your product instantly legible.
- Innovate where it differentiates value, not on standard plumbing (login, search, carts). Reinventing a date picker costs more than it earns.
- When you must diverge, signal it clearly and ease the transition — unexpected patterns trigger confusion and errors.
